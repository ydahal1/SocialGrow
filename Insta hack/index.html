Instructions

  1. Go to instagram.com on Google Chrome browser and log in
  2. Search for the account with followers of your interest
  3. Open Dev tools or right click on the page and click inspect
  4. Click on console tab. This is where you will see the progress
  5. Click on followers and scroll down as far as you can.
  6. This is a slow and organic process. Don't worry about scrolling all the way to the end of the follow list.
  7. Now click the button below labeled PERFORM MAGIC. It will take few minutes for the program to start
  8. Once you see PROCESS RUNNING on the console, leave your computer or atleast that window or tab alone.
  9. Make a note of how many followers you have now so you can compare the progress later
